Managing Kevin The Monkey

Just imagine, you’re managing an artist with the most played song on the internet.

How does this work?

Who is the artist?

Kevin The Monkey is a virtual recording artist. 

His first single just happens to be the most played song on the internet, “Monkeys Spinning Monkeys” written by legendary composer Kevin MacLeod (140 billion plays).
‍
The problem is no-one knows who Kevin The Monkey is, and that’s where YOU, THE MANAGERS come in.
‍
Kevin The Monkey has a cheeky, but starry personality and boundless musical talent.

How do you become the manager?

Adopt Kevin The Monkey in his first NFT collection.

How can a virtual character be an ACTUAL artist?

Kevin, a virtual character, has backing from world class musicians, artists and animators.
 
He will become a singing, animated 3D character, collaborating with some of the biggest artists in the world. 

Has Kevin The Monkey released any songs?

Yes, and it is the most played song on the internet, "Monkeys Spinning Monkeys" by Kevin MacLeod. It has 140 billion plays, currently doing 1 billion a week. 

MacLeod has entrusted Kevin The Monkey to be the public facing artist.

Never heard of it? Of course you have. Stephen Fry can explain more here.

How can the manager make an impact?

Kevin The Monkey holders will act as Kevin’s Managers, voting on key creative and business decisions, like single choices or brand partnerships.

Do the managers get rewarded?

Yes. 
‍
As commission for the manager's hard work, 20% of NFT primary and secondary sales will form a treasury. This will be used to buy back a portion of Kevin The Monkey NFTs and then burned, as a reward for the Kevin hitting targets. ie - a BBC Radio 1 Breakfast play, 200 billion plays on TikTok - more information to come.

What does the future look like?

Kevin is a recording artist so will be releasing songs, music videos, perform live and collaborate with some of the most talented people in music and art, from up n’ comers to more established names. 
‍
Kevin will bridge the gap for web2 and web3 artists, educating and creating an equal economic playing-field for songwriters, producers, singers and visual artists.

About the Project

What is an NFT?

NFT stands for ‘non-fungible token’, which means that it's a one-of-a-kind digital asset that belongs to you and you only. It is a digital certificate of authenticity proving both the creation and ownership of a digital asset. The clever thing about NFTs is that once you hold one in your wallet they can act like a membership pass which can interact with all sorts of cool things on the internet, like private Discord channels, via the magic and wizardry of has become known as ‘Web3’.

Why Ethereum?

Ethereum is the main network for NFTs and home of all of the biggest blue-chip NFT projects (including most but not all of Kevin’s more OG primate cousins), while there are NFT marketplaces on other blockchains, the main party is happening on Ethereum so that’s where Kevin wants to be.

What is Metamask?

Metamask is the best known and most secure and respected Ethereum wallet which is where the smart contracts that define ownership of your NFTs will be stored safely. NEVER give your Metamask password to anyone (especially anyone asking for it on Discord) and make sure to write down your seed phrase somewhere safe and secure so if you do lose your password or your computer breaks you’ll be able to regenerate your wallet and its contents.
‍
Each Metamask has a public address which you can copy and paste and send publicly to people without worry. Anyone with this address can then send you other NFTs or supported crypto currencies. If you win a pre-sale list space we will ask you to enter your wallet address into a channel on discord.

Is Kevin MacLeod on board?

Yes, Kevin knows all about the project and fully supports it. Although he is not part of the core team, we run ideas by him and it is important to us that he agrees with the decisions we make.
